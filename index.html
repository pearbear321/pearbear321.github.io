Heirloom Finder

This tool will help you find good heirlooms, it will probably be most useful during stages where you're trying to find good heirlooms of the next higher rarity than the ones you have.

For example, if you can do voids at 230+, but normally do them at 215 because of the challenge that ends there; you put 215 in the first box and 230 in the second one. Then hit the search for heirlooms button and it will tell you how many voids to do at 215 followed by more how many more at 230 to get the maximum rarity available at 230.
